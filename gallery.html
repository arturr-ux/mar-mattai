Mar Mattai Monastery Digitalization

Mar Mattai Monastery is one of the most important monasteries in Iraq founded in 363. However, conflicts and problems occurred during the past years leading to the erasure of most of its features. The inscriptions and decorations are barely present now. Hence, our project aims to 3D scan the monastery and build a 3D model to preserve it for the next generations.
